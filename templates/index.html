About

This project all started because of my mom. Growing up in a Mexican household, I would find myself scratching my head at some of the Mexican sayings or "dichos" she would use. Some are funny, some are deep and some frankly just dont make sense. The literal translations from Spanish to English always make me laugh, but the message for the most part is received. These are my versions of "motivational" quotes, the ones I grew up hearing, that some of you guys out there heard your jefa relate to you growing up.

My name is Monica Ramirez, this is dedicated to my mom, Crisogona. To my family, who always reminds me: "Ponte las pilas!".
